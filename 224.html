Inside Aerojet: The Essential Services That Keep Cargo Aviation Operating 24/7

2025-02-01 · 6 min read · Aerojet Editorial Team · Central America

Behind every cargo aircraft that lands, loads, and takes off on time, there is a highly coordinated network of specialists, machinery, and nonstop operations. At Aerojet, these essential services run 24/7 to ensure that logistics chains never stop moving.

1. Ramp Operations: Precision at Every Turn

Cargo aviation depends on a perfectly synchronized ramp team. Tractors, loaders, GPUs, and safety marshals all work in unison to move aircraft quickly and safely. At Aerojet, certified crews manage marshalling, parking, and aircraft servicing with minute-by-minute precision.

Our use of electric loaders and towbarless tractors has also reduced emissions while improving operational efficiency—supporting both sustainability and performance.

2. Specialized Cargo Handling & Cold Chain Control

From perishables to pharmaceuticals, cargo operations require strict temperature management and traceability. Aerojet’s teams ensure controlled environments, proper documentation, and safe movement for all types of sensitive freight.

Our bonded warehouse hub in Panama strengthens regional logistics by allowing short-term storage and rapid redistribution.

3. Dispatch, Permits & Real-Time Coordination

No cargo flight moves without an operations center working behind the scenes. Dispatch teams issue flight plans, verify NOTAMs, coordinate slots, and maintain communication with ATC and pilots.

Whether arranging overflight permits or assisting with AOG situations, Aerojet provides full operational continuity across multiple countries.

4. AVSEC: Protecting Every Shipment

Security is a critical component of cargo aviation. Through our AVSEC division, we provide international-grade screening, cargo supervision, and preventive risk management aligned with ICAO standards.

Every operation is overseen by certified personnel who ensure safe and compliant handling from arrival to departure.

5. 24/7 Crew & Logistics Support

Cargo crews often operate irregular or overnight schedules, requiring fast solutions. Aerojet manages hotel arrangements, ground transportation, catering, and on-call assistance at any hour.

With continuous communication between stations and dispatch, operators receive real-time updates for a seamless operational flow.
