Inside Aerojet: The Essential Services Keeping Cargo Aviation Running 24/7

2025-02-14 · 6 min read · Aerojet Editorial Team · Costa Rica, Central America

Cargo doesn’t sleep — and neither do we. At Aerojet, a 24/7 operational model ensures that every shipment, from perishables to high-value freight, moves with speed, safety, and precision across Central America.

24/7 Ramp Operations: The Beating Heart of Cargo Aviation

Behind every cargo flight, a synchronized team moves into action the moment wheels touch the ground. Ramp agents, supervisors, GSE operators, and safety coordinators work with one goal: achieve a flawless turnaround.

From marshalling and ULD handling to real-time safety checks, Aerojet’s ramp teams operate with precision and rapid coordination. Even at 3 a.m., the airfield is alive with turbine hum, headlights, and orchestrated movement.

Specialized equipment like electric loaders, towbarless tractors, and GPU/ASU systems guarantee efficiency while reducing environmental impact — an innovation Aerojet pioneered in Central America.

Cold Chain & Sensitive Cargo Management

Pharmaceuticals, perishables, biotech shipments, and temperature-controlled goods require more than speed; they demand accuracy and strict thermal protection.

Aerojet’s cold-chain processes include temperature monitoring, certified handling procedures, and specialized equipment to prevent exposure during transfer windows.

Whether it’s -20°C vaccines or fresh produce destined for international markets, maintaining integrity is a non-negotiable standard.

  • Temperature-controlled storage
  • Pharma-compliant handling
  • Time-sensitive processing
  • Continuous monitoring and digital logs

Documentation, Customs & Compliance Without Delays

Cargo flights depend on precise paperwork — airway bills, manifests, dangerous goods declarations, customs forms, and more. A single error can delay an entire operation.

Aerojet’s documentation specialists work around the clock to coordinate with customs authorities, freight forwarders, and operators, ensuring every shipment clears on time.

The result: faster rotations, fewer holds, and transparent communication for every stakeholder.

Fueling, Technical Support & AOG Response

For cargo airlines operating tight schedules, fueling and technical readiness must happen quickly and safely. Aerojet coordinates Jet A-1 supply, performs safety oversight, and assists with quality checks.

FAA-certified mechanics support line maintenance and AOG troubleshooting, reducing downtime and ensuring safer, more reliable operations — day or night.

These services allow cargo flights to maintain continuity even during unexpected technical challenges.

Operations Control: The 24/7 Nerve Center

The true engine behind Aerojet’s cargo division is the operations desk — a nonstop command center connecting dispatchers, ATC, crew, and ground teams in real time.

This hub manages flight plans, pre-alerts, delays, load data, special cargo requests, and safety coordination, guaranteeing seamless communication no matter the hour.

With round-the-clock monitoring, operators can anticipate challenges before they impact the flight.
